Installations and Conceptual Art

"In Case of Emergency LIKE this Page" was installed at Waiheke Island's Headland Sculpture on the Gulf in 2011.  It comments on the ease of a "click" "LIKE" or "share" in a natural disaster. Click on the link to read about the full intent of the work.
Another comment on the digital age, is the Barrel of Monkeys installation at ShapeShifter in Wellington 2016. A simple reminder that children still need fun interaction with their parents and peers that is removed from the relative ease of digital and virtual games
